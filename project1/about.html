About Coach Sol

Coach Sol is a record holding D1 track athlete from central NC

Currently the 2022-2023 UNCA Track Team Field Captian

He holds multiple facility records as well as the winning 2023 indoor NCAA Mens Shotput Big South Championship

Sol has been throwing for almost a decade and has coached people of all ages and backgrounds

Ranked number 4 in New Zealand where he holds dual citizenship, he currently has a PR of 18.29m
